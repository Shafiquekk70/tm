FOOTBALL

THE BEAUTIFUL GAME

Football is the world's most popular sport, played by over 250 million players in more than 200 countries. It's a game of skill, strategy, and passion.

BASIC INFORMATION

Number of Players

11 players per team on the field, with 3-7 substitutes allowed depending on the competition.

Match Duration

90 minutes (two halves of 45 minutes) with added time for stoppages. Extra time of 30 minutes if needed.

Main Strategy

Formation tactics, ball possession, counter-attacking, set pieces, and team coordination.

Field Dimensions

Length: 100-110m, Width: 64-75m. Goal size: 7.32m wide × 2.44m high.

GAME STRATEGY

Formations

  • 4-4-2: Classic balanced formation
  • 4-3-3: Attacking with wingers
  • 3-5-2: Strong midfield control
  • 4-2-3-1: Modern flexible system
  • 5-3-2: Defensive counter-attack

Playing Styles

  • Tiki-Taka: Short passing and possession (Barcelona style)
  • Counter-Attack: Quick transitions from defense to attack
  • High Press: Pressing opponents high up the pitch
  • Park the Bus: Ultra-defensive strategy
  • Total Football: Positional flexibility

RULES OF THE GAME

Basic Rules

  • No hands except for goalkeeper in penalty area
  • Offside rule: Attacker must have 2 opponents between them and goal
  • Throw-ins for ball out on sidelines
  • Goal kicks and corner kicks for ball out on end lines
  • Direct and indirect free kicks for fouls

Fouls & Cards

  • Yellow Card: Warning for serious fouls
  • Red Card: Immediate ejection for violent conduct
  • Penalty: For fouls inside penalty area
  • VAR: Video Assistant Referee for key decisions

IMPORTANT FACTS

History & Origin

  • Origin: Ancient China (Cuju), Greece, Rome
  • Modern Football: England 1863 (FA founded)
  • First World Cup: 1930 in Uruguay
  • FIFA Founded: 1904 in Paris

Major Tournaments

  • FIFA World Cup: Every 4 years
  • UEFA Champions League: Annual club competition
  • UEFA European Championship: Every 4 years
  • Copa America: South American championship
  • Premier League: Top English league
