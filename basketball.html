BASKETBALL

THE FAST-PACED GAME

Basketball is a team sport in which two teams of five players each try to score points by shooting a ball through a hoop elevated 10 feet above the ground.

BASIC INFORMATION

Number of Players

5 players per team on the court at a time. Each team has 7-12 players on roster.

Game Duration

NBA: 48 minutes (4 quarters of 12 min) | FIBA: 40 minutes (4 quarters of 10 min)

Main Strategy

Pick-and-roll, fast breaks, zone defense, isolation plays, and three-point shooting.

GAME STRATEGY

Offensive Strategies

  • Pick and Roll: Most common play in basketball
  • Fast Break: Quick transition from defense to offense
  • Triangle Offense: Creating passing triangles
  • Motion Offense: Constant player movement
  • Isolation: One-on-one play for star players

Defensive Strategies

  • Man-to-Man: Each player guards specific opponent
  • Zone Defense: Players guard specific areas
  • Full Court Press: Defense across entire court
  • Double Team: Two defenders guard one offensive player
  • Box-and-One: Combination of zone and man defense

RULES OF THE GAME

Basic Rules

  • Dribbling required to move with the ball
  • 24-second shot clock in NBA (30 seconds in college)
  • 8-second half court violation
  • 3-second rule in the paint
  • 5-second closely guarded rule

Fouls & Violations

  • Personal Foul: Illegal physical contact
  • Technical Foul: Unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Traveling: Moving without dribbling
  • Double Dribble: Dribbling with two hands or stopping then restarting
  • Goaltending: Interfering with ball on downward flight

SCORING SYSTEM

Points System

  • 3 Points: Shot made from beyond three-point line
  • 2 Points: Shot made from inside three-point line
  • 1 Point: Each successful free throw
  • And-1: Additional free throw after made basket with foul

Game Flow

  • Tip-off: Game starts with jump ball
  • Quarters/Periods: 4 quarters with breaks
  • Halftime: 15-minute break after 2nd quarter
  • Overtime: 5 minutes if game tied after regulation
  • Timeouts: 7 per team in NBA (full & 20-second)

IMPORTANT FACTS

History & Origin

  • Invented: 1891 by Dr. James Naismith
  • First Game: December 21, 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts
  • First NBA Game: November 1, 1946 (Toronto vs New York)
  • NBA Founded: 1946 as BAA, became NBA in 1949
  • First Basketball: Used soccer ball and peach baskets

Major Tournaments

  • NBA Finals: Annual championship series
  • FIBA Basketball World Cup: Every 4 years
  • Olympic Basketball: Since 1936 (men), 1976 (women)
  • EuroLeague: Top European club competition
  • NCAA March Madness: US college basketball tournament
